Hello! My name is Kristy and I’m a freelancing traditional and fiber artist working her way through the journey called life.

At a young age I spent much of my free time drawing, mostly inspired by various anime at the time such as Sailor Moon and Cardcaptor Sakura. After which I focused my attention on graphic and web design, but I was never truly happy. After many years of creating web designs, I realized that my true passion is illustration, and now needle felting as well.

I adore crafts, from plush making, sewing, and anything related to DIY; to be able to create something with a few tools and materials is something that I will never tire of. As such, I’m always willing and wanting to learn as much as I can, experimenting and trying different techniques.

Recently I have started creating and designing creatures of various designs, in addition to my original characters, that often times sparks inspiration for the next painting or fiber art.

In addition to illustration and needle felting, I have a fondness for Asian Ball Jointed Dolls. They are a perfect medium that combines all the things I love, from sewing clothes to creating unique props and assets for them.

My illustrations are simply a starting point, and I only want to move forward in my career!
